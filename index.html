Common Problems

Why NPS gets stuck for so many people?

If the portal is rejecting your requests or your PRAN is frozen, it's usually a structural error. We specialise in resolving these specific frictions.

💼

Job Changes

PRAN freezes during shifts between sectors or roles — government to corporate and back.

🌍

Global Moves

Updating bank details and NRI status to allow overseas access to your accumulated corpus.

⚙️

Technical Rejections

Valid balances being blocked by opaque portal errors — KYC mismatches, expired OTPs, and more.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Succession

Helping beneficiaries and legal heirs access funds with dignity after a subscriber passes.

Our Process

How we work

1

Diagnostic Scan — Paid & Confidential

2

Institutional Coordination

We correct records, update bank and PRAN details, and file the right forms with the CRA, NPS Trust, and nodal offices — so you don't have to.

3

End-to-End Resolution

We stay with your case until your funds become accessible. Milestone-based billing means you only pay for real progress.
